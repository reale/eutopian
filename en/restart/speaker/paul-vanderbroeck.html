Paul Vanderbroeck

Dr Paul Vanderbroeck (CH/NL) studied history and sociology in the NL, France and the USA. After an international career as an executive in Human Resource Management, he is now an Executive Coach specialized in Leadership Development. He has been teaching at LUISS Business School and the University of Lausanne.
